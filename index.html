Welcome to my art page!

Who am I?

Hello everyone! My name is Maxine Derksen and I'm a creative mind from the Netherlands. I am currently living in Eindhoven, where I study Human-Technology Interaction at the TU/e.

As this page already suggests, I like to get creative in my spare time! Please have a look at my works in the gallery if you're interested in what I do.

What do I do?

There is not really one type of material that I prefer, but at the moment I'm very much into linoprinting. I like the simplicity and the different types of steps in the progress.

Other than printing, I like to paint with aquarel, draw with pastel pencils or bake some pottery. You'll always find me trying something new!
